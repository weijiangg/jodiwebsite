Interview With Christina Ahn

Residence Hall Association Vice President

Christina, a sophomore and Vice President of the Residence Hall Association (RHA), leads efforts to enhance campus community experiences. With a focus on advocacy and event planning, RHA strives to address safety concerns through initiatives. Christina’s vision is to create a cohesive community atmosphere among the diverse on-campus population, fostering connections and friendships among students living in different residence halls. She plays a key role in mentoring the 13 governors and ensuring effective community governance to achieve this vision.

“My vision for RHA in the broader campus community is to enhance a sense of community.”

— Christina Ahn

The Future

Christina for RHA President

Why is Christina Running for 2024-2025 RHA President?

Christina shares why she is running for president.

Finding Community. Diversity in Leadership. Carolina = HOME

Christina wanted to meet new people as a first-year and decided to run for Craige Community Governor! She met her closest friends through Craige Community Government. She knows that this can be a positive experience for all residential students.

Christina wants to encourage her fellow UNC-CH students that anyone is capable of holding a leadership position. Everyone has something unique to offer and RHA provided her with the platform to do so.

UNC students are ambitious, hard working students who strive for excellence. In a fast paced environment like UNC-CH, students deserve a place to relax. Christina has the passion and need to further amplify the feeling of home in all our residential communities on campus.

“I want to create a clear image of home for all Carolina students! Through community, transparency, and safety, we can make this happen!” — Christina Ahn
