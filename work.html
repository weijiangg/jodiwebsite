Interview With Christina Ahn

Residence Hall Association Vice President

Christina, a sophomore and Vice President of the Residence Hall Association
(RHA), leads efforts to enhance campus community experiences. With a focus
on advocacy and event planning, RHA strives to address safety concerns
through initiatives. Christina's vision is to create a cohesive community
atmosphere among the diverse on-campus population, fostering connections
and friendships among students living in different residence halls. She plays a
key role in mentoring the 13 governors and ensuring effective community
governance to achieve this vision.

“My vision for RHA in the broader campus
community is to enhance a sense of community.”
— Christina Ahn
